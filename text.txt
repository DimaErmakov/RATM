Open a new file in VSCode.
Type lorem and press Tab. This will generate a predefined Lorem Ipsum text snippet.
You can control the length of the generated text by specifying a number after lorem. For example, lorem5 will generate five words of Lorem Ipsum text.
If you need a specific number of paragraphs, you can use lorem[number of paragraphs]. For example, lorem3 will generate three paragraphs of Lorem Ipsum text.
